Our Shop And Cafe

At Sunnyhill Farm, our cattle and sheep are bred and reared naturally in the beautiful Clyde Valley. Choose from a wide range of beef, veal, lamb, and chicken. We also supply a variety of cold meats. We grow a wide variety of seasonal fruit and vegetables. When these are out of season, they are available frozen, so you can enjoy them all year round. We also supply produce from other parts of the country and the rest of the world.
We have a small café attached to the farm shop so people can enjoy their purchases immediately. Some of the most popular items in a farm shop are:

Eat

Our delicious and nutritious menu features seasonal produce from the farm and local producers. We always try to have interesting vegan and gluten free options available. Our Cafe is open throughout the year for breakfast, lunch, hot drinks and sweet treats. We can cater for all dietary requirements and guarantee you will always find something on our menu to tempt you.

Bakery & sweet treats

There’s nothing quite like the smell of freshly baked bread and that’s what you can expect when you visit our Scottish farm shop first thing in the morning We bake the very best farmhouse bread every day along with our fantastic range of delicious homemade scones, traybakes and occasion cakes. We can cater to a range of dietary requirements and can even make personalised birthday cakes for your loved one, so get in touch if you want us to create something special. To tempt our customers further, we have handmade chocolates as well as award-winning ice-creams.
