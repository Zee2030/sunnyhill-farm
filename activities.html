Our Activities

Little Farmers at Sunnyhill

Did you know that we have an adventure farm park, Little Farmers, at Sunnyhill? Our family-friendly attraction is ready to welcome your budding little farmers to enjoy fields of homegrown fun down at Sunnyhill Farm.
There’s so much to do! You can meet and feed the animals, get busy with indoor and outdoor play, cost effective kids’ birthday parties and enjoy the country air and good farm-fresh food!

Christmas Event

Experience the enchantment of Christmas like never before at Sunnyhill’ Santa Adventure, an extraordinary Christmas event that will transport you to a world of twinkling lights, festive wonder, and heartwarming moments.
Christmas is back at Sunnyhill Farm bigger and better than ever! Santa Claus, in his eagerness to prepare for the grand Christmas Eve journey, has encountered a problem of epic proportions.
He’s lost the magical word that powers the start of his sleigh! Now, it’s up to you to join this magical quest and help Santa recover the missing word.

School & Nursery Visits

Little Farmers is one of the most exciting venues for children’s parties near Edinburgh. We have three party rooms to cater to both small and big kids parties, all looking out onto the farmyard and surrounding countryside.
Kids can enjoy a day of farm-filled fun with a tasty selection of healthy yet delicious food and snacks from our Farm, meet-and-greet with our resident farm animals not to mention exploring our epic play barn! Let us tailor the perfect party for your Little Farmers at Suunyhill!
